The problem we solve

When an AI report generator says "lesion on left frontal" but the truth is "lesion on right frontal", off-the-shelf BioClinicalBERT scores it 0.99 ("looks great"). NeuroVal-3D catches the laterality flip and scores it 0.18 (FLAGGED).
